About

For my summer reading book I read "Berserk". For my summer reading book I read "Berserk". "Berserk" is a fantasy manga and anime series created by Kentaro Miura. The story centers on Guts, a formidable lone mercenary known for his incredible strength and skill in battle. Born from tragic circumstances and raised in a hostile environment. "Berserk" delves into profound themes such as fate versus free will, the nature of humanity, and the psychological toll of violence. Guts' internal struggles and moral dilemmas resonate deeply, making him a complex and relatable protagonist. The series is renowned for its stunning artwork, intricate world-building, and its ability to tackle dark and mature themes, solidifying its place as a seminal work in the dark fantasy genre.
 

About The Author

Kentaro Miura was a renowned Japanese manga artist best known for creating the critically acclaimed dark fantasy series "Berserk." Born on July 11, 1966, Miura began his career as a manga artist in the late 1980s and gained fame for his intricate artwork, complex characters, and deep storytelling. "Berserk," which debuted in 1989, explores themes of fate, free will, and the human condition, earning a dedicated fanbase worldwide. Miura's work has had a profound influence on the manga and anime industries, and he is celebrated for his artistic talent and storytelling prowess. He passed away on May 6, 2021, leaving behind a lasting legacy.
